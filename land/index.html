Once upon a time

Something magical unfolded in the universe
and Willow found herself in a whole new world,
where everything looked strangely familiar,
yet uniquely different.

The Encounter 

Lost in the depths of the dark forest,
Willows eyes were drawn to a sparkle
of light. Suddenly, a shiny creature
emerged before her - Wisp, appearing
only to those with unique purposes.

Path of the unknown

In this new, strange world where everyone
communicated through sign languages,
Willow was faced with a challenge unlike any other.
But fear not, for she was no longer alone

The beginning of something magical

Offering guidance, Wisp became
Willows steadfast companion,
illuminating their path through the
shadows on their extraordinary journey.
